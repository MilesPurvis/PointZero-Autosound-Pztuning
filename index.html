What is Time Attack?

Motortrend: What Is Time Attack
"Time attack racing is a type of motorsport where the racers compete for the best lap time. Each vehicle is timed through circuits of the track. The racers make a preliminary circuit, then run the timed laps, and then finish with a cool-down lap. Time attack and time trial events differ by competition format and rules. Time attack has a limited number of laps, time trial has open sessions"
